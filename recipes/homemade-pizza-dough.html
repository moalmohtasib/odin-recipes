Homemade Pizza Dough

Description

This homemade pizza dough recipe is easy to make and yields a delicious, crispy crust. With just a few simple ingredients, you can have fresh, homemade pizza in no time. Top with your favorite toppings and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water (110°F)
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the yeast, warm water, and sugar. Let the mixture sit for 5-10 minutes, or until foamy.
  2. Add the flour, salt, and olive oil to the mixing bowl and stir until the dough comes together.
  3. Transfer the dough to a floured surface and knead for 5-10 minutes, or until the dough is smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl and cover with a damp towel. Let the dough rise in a warm, draft-free place for 1-2 hours, or until it has doubled in size.
  5. Punch down the dough and roll it out to your desired thickness. Add your favorite toppings and bake at 450°F for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown.
  6. Slice and serve hot.
