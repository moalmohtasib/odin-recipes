Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

These classic chocolate chip cookies are a favorite for good reason - they're soft, chewy, and packed with chocolate chips. The recipe is easy to make and yields perfectly delicious cookies every time.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 3/4 cup white granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, beat together butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy. Add eggs and vanilla extract and beat until well combined.
  4. Add flour mixture to butter mixture and mix until just combined. Stir in chocolate chips.
  5. Drop spoonfuls of cookie dough onto prepared baking sheets. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until cookies are golden brown.
  6. Cool cookies on baking sheets for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
