Garlic Mashed Potatoes

Description

Garlic mashed potatoes are a classic side dish that pairs perfectly with a variety of main courses. This recipe takes plain mashed potatoes to the next level by adding in plenty of garlic for a delicious and savory flavor. Creamy, buttery, and packed with garlic, these mashed potatoes are sure to be a hit with your family or guests!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Place the potatoes and minced garlic in a large pot of salted water. Bring to a boil and cook for 15-20 minutes, or until the potatoes are tender.
  2. Drain the potatoes and garlic and return them to the pot.
  3. Add the milk and butter to the pot and mash the potatoes and garlic until smooth and creamy.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Transfer the mashed potatoes to a serving bowl and serve hot.
