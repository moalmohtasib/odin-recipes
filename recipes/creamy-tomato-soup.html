Creamy Tomato Soup

Description

This creamy tomato soup is the ultimate comfort food. It's made with fresh tomatoes, cream, and a blend of spices for a rich, satisfying flavor. Serve it with a grilled cheese sandwich for the perfect cozy meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1 cup chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream

Steps

  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onions and garlic and saut√© until softened, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add chopped tomatoes, basil, oregano, salt, and black pepper. Stir to combine and cook for 10-15 minutes, or until tomatoes have softened.
  3. Remove from heat and use an immersion blender or transfer to a blender to puree until smooth.
  4. Return soup to the pot and add chicken or vegetable broth. Bring to a simmer and let cook for 10 minutes.
  5. Stir in heavy cream and let simmer for an additional 5 minutes, or until heated through.
  6. Adjust seasoning to taste and serve hot.
