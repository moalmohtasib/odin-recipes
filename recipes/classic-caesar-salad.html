Classic Caesar Salad

Description

This classic Caesar salad recipe is a crowd-pleaser that's perfect for any occasion. Crisp romaine lettuce, crunchy croutons, and salty Parmesan cheese come together in a tangy dressing made with garlic, anchovy paste, and lemon juice. Whether you're looking for a light lunch, a side dish for dinner, or a potluck contribution, this salad is sure to impress. Plus, it's easy to customize by adding grilled chicken, shrimp, or other toppings to make it your own.

Ingredients

  • 1 head of romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/2 cup croutons
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon anchovy paste
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the olive oil, Dijon mustard, garlic, anchovy paste, lemon juice, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  2. Add the chopped romaine lettuce and croutons to the bowl and toss to coat with the dressing.
  3. Divide the salad among four plates and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
  4. Serve immediately.
